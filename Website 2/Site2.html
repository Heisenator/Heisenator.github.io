About us

Based in Kent, Hot Beans work with businesses to create innovative and effective design solutions. With our passion for modern web design and graphic design trends, coupled with an awareness of the traditional aspects of design such as typography and composition, we have the skills, creativity and resources to deliver the highest level of promotional support for your company.

By giving maximum effort to every client and with our commitment to graphic design and the projects we work on, we ensure your corporate identity works consistently across all of your advertising media. Our years in the business and accumulated knowledge of print production means we match the highest levels of creativity to individual objectives, budget and schedules.
